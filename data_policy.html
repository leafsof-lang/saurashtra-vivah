Palkar Vivah & Connect — Data Policy

Last updated: October 28, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Palkar Vivah & Connect, developed and managed by Leafsof Technologies. This Data Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how long we keep it, and how you can control or delete your data.

2. Data We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use the app:

  • Personal information: name, email address, phone number, gender, date of birth, and other profile fields you provide.
  • User content: profile photos, bio, interests, hobbies, and messages you send to other users.
  • Location: city/state if you choose to share it. We do not share precise real-time GPS by default unless you explicitly enable a feature that requires it.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To create and manage your account and profile.
  • To provide matchmaking and communication features.
  • To enable messaging and profile viewing between users.
  • To improve our service, perform analytics, and fix bugs.
  • To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from authorities.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Other users as part of the app’s matchmaking and communication features.
  • Service providers who support our operations (e.g., analytics or notifications) and comply with strict privacy standards.
  • Legal or regulatory authorities when required by law.

5. Security

All communication between the app and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS to encrypt data in transit. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data, including access controls and encrypted backups where appropriate. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • We retain personal data while your account is active.
  • If you request account deletion, we will remove your profile, photos, messages, and other personal data within 30 days, unless otherwise required to retain certain records for legal or financial compliance (e.g., payment records).
  • Aggregated or anonymized data used for analytics may be retained indefinitely.
How to delete your account
  1. Open the Palkar Vivah & Connect app → Settings → Delete Account, OR
  2. Send an email to leafsof@gmail.com with the subject “Delete my account” and include your registered phone number.

After your request, we will confirm by email and complete the removal within the timeframe above.

7. User Controls

  • You can update or remove profile fields directly in the app.
  • Block or report other users via the profile or chat interface. Reported content will be reviewed by our moderation team.
  • To request a copy or deletion of your data, contact leafsof@gmail.com.

8. Children

This app is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, contact us and we will delete it immediately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Policy periodically. When we make changes, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
