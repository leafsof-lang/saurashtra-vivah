Saurashtra Vivah — Data Policy

Keeping your privacy clear and simple • Last updated: September 5, 2025

Welcome — this page explains what we collect, why, and what control you have over your information.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Saurashtra Vivah. This Data Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and share it, how long we keep it, and how you can control or delete your data.

2. Data we collect

We may collect the following types of data when you use the app:

  • Personal information: name, email, phone number, gender, date of birth, and other profile fields you provide.
  • User content: profile photos, bio, interests, hobbies, and messages you send to other users.
  • Location: city/state (we do not share precise GPS by default unless you enable a location-based feature).

3. How we use your data

  • To create and manage your account and profile.
  • To provide matchmaking, messaging, and profile-view features.
  • To improve our service through analytics and bug fixes (aggregated/anonymized).
  • To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data only when required (e.g., legal requests).

5. Security

All traffic between the app and our servers is encrypted with HTTPS/TLS. We use industry-standard safeguards such as access controls and encrypted backups where appropriate. No system is completely risk-free, so we continuously monitor and improve security.

6. Data retention & deletion

  • We retain personal data while your account is active.
  • If you request deletion, we remove profile, photos, messages and personal data within 30 days, except where required to retain certain records for legal or financial compliance.
  • Aggregated or anonymized analytics data may be retained indefinitely.
How to delete your account
  1. Open the Saurashtra Vivah app → Settings → Delete Account, OR
  2. Email leafsof@gmail.com with subject "Delete my account" and include your registered phone number.

After your request we'll confirm by email and complete removal within the timeframe above (unless retention is required by law).

7. User controls

  • Update or remove profile fields from within the app.
  • Block or report other users via profile or chat; reported content is reviewed by moderation.
  • Request copies of your data or specific deletions by emailing leafsof@gmail.com.

8. Children

The service is for users 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18 — contact us if you believe otherwise and we'll delete it.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. When we do, we'll update the "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes means you accept the new policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or requests, email leafsof@gmail.com.
