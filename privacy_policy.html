This Privacy Policy describes how Saurashtra Vivah (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our application or services. By using the app, you agree to the terms outlined below.

4. Data Processing

Some data (e.g., OTP verification) may be processed ephemerally — meaning it is handled in memory and not stored permanently. Other data, such as your profile and photos, are stored securely in our systems until deleted.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information only when required by law, or when necessary to protect our rights or users’ safety.

6. Data Retention

  • Anonymized or aggregated data may be retained indefinitely for analytics.

7. Your Rights

  • You can update or delete profile fields directly in the app.
  • You can request account deletion through the app or by contacting us via email.
  • You may request a copy of your personal data by contacting us.

8. Children

Our services are intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.
