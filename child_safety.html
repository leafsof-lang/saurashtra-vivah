Child Safety & Protection Standards

Palkar Vivah & Connect — Published standards to prevent and respond to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE/CSAM).

1. Our Commitment

Palkar Vivah & Connect strictly prohibits any content, activity, or behaviour that promotes, depicts, or facilitates child sexual abuse or exploitation. We are committed to protecting minors and complying with all applicable laws, regulations, and platform safety standards.

2. Age Requirements

  • All users must be at least 18 years old to register for Palkar Vivah & Connect.
  • We collect date of birth during registration. Accounts with suspicious or inconsistent age information are manually reviewed and may be suspended or deleted.

3. Prevention & Detection

  • Automated systems: Our tools flag potentially exploitative content or suspicious activity for human review.
  • Manual moderation: A dedicated moderation team investigates flagged reports and escalates confirmed violations.
  • Proactive review: We periodically audit reported and public content to identify and act against policy violations.

4. Reporting Mechanisms (In-App)

Users can report safety concerns directly within the app via the Report option on profiles, chats, or content. All reports related to child safety are prioritized for review and immediate action.

If a child is in immediate danger: Contact your local emergency services first, then report the incident to us for internal review and escalation.

5. Cooperation with Authorities

We fully comply with applicable child protection and cybercrime laws. Verified CSAE/CSAM incidents are promptly reported to relevant national or regional authorities. We preserve necessary records and metadata for law enforcement when legally required.

6. Enforcement

  • Accounts found in violation of our child protection standards will be immediately suspended or permanently banned.
  • All related content is removed, and relevant data may be retained securely for investigation purposes.

7. Training & Internal Policies

Our moderators and staff receive training on identifying CSAE/CSAM material, handling user reports sensitively, and maintaining legal compliance. We maintain internal escalation and response protocols aligned with industry best practices.
